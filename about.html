About PetTastic

Learn more about our story and mission

Our Mission

At PetTastic, our mission is to create high-quality, durable, and safe toys that bring joy to pets and peace of mind to their owners. We believe that playtime is essential for the physical and mental wellbeing of all animals, and we're committed to making toys that enhance that experience.

Our toys are designed to engage, stimulate, and entertain pets of all types and sizes, while meeting the highest standards of safety and sustainability. We're passionate about creating products that pets love and owners trust.

Our Story

PetTastic was born from a simple observation: while there were many pet toys on the market, few combined durability, safety, and fun in equal measure. As pet owners ourselves, we found that toys either didn't last, weren't safe, or failed to truly engage our pets.

Founded in 2018 by Sarah and Mike Johnson, lifelong animal lovers with three dogs and two cats of their own, PetTastic started with a simple prototype of a rope toy designed to withstand even the most enthusiastic chewers. From that initial design, we expanded to a full range of toys for various pets, all tested and approved by our own furry family members.

What began as a small operation in our garage has grown into a beloved brand trusted by thousands of pet owners nationwide. Despite our growth, we remain committed to our founding principles: creating toys that are durable, safe, eco-friendly, and, most importantly, fun for pets of all kinds.
