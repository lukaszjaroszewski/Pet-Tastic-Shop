Frequently Asked Questions

What is your shipping policy?

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days) and expedited shipping (1-2 business days) options. Orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping. International shipping is available to select countries.

How do I return a product?

If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange. Please contact our customer service team to initiate the return process.

Are your toys safe for all pets?

All our toys undergo rigorous safety testing and are made from non-toxic materials. However, we recommend selecting toys appropriate for your pet's size, age, and play style. Always supervise your pet during playtime.

Do you offer wholesale options?

Yes, we offer wholesale options for pet stores and other retailers. Please contact our sales team at wholesale@pettastic.com for more information and to set up a wholesale account.
